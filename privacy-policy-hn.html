Privacy Policy for Humble HN Chrome Extension

This privacy policy describes how the Humble HN Chrome extension ("the extension") collects, uses, and shares user data.

Data Collection and Use:

  • The extension does not collect any personal or sensitive user data.
  • The extension does not transmit any user data to external servers or third parties.
  • The extension only interacts with the Hacker News website (https://news.ycombinator.com/) to modify the appearance of specific elements on the page.

Data Sharing:

The extension does not share any user data with any parties.
